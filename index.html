ABOUT US

Shivani Mobile Center is a wholesale store that provides its customers mobiles phones and its connections, accessories, recharges and it also help you to get finance for your wish listed products too. The mobile center opened its doors for customers in 2013 in Sohawal Chauraha, Faizabad. It was founded and promoted by Ajay Gupta with his values to spread happiness to the employees, customers as well as the stake holders.

The price of basic smart phone starts approximately from Rs. 4000. It tries to understand customers need and serve them the best product in their range. The biggest strength of their mobile center is their well trained staff.
